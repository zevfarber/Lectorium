Instructions
English in the top bar shows the English beside the original for the whole text; while it's on, choose natural or literal there to switch every sentence at once. Click a sentence to focus it — the buttons always show what you'll get next: 🔊 Listen becomes ⏹ Stop while it reads (Esc also stops); 🐢 Slow plays it slowly and becomes 🐇 Regular; EN reveals the English and becomes hide EN; ≈ literal and ≈ natural switch between the two English versions for that one sentence (natural is shown first); 📝 opens the grammar note. ✍️ Try lets you attempt your own translation (typed or dictated 🎤) before comparing with the English. Click any word for its meaning and pronunciation. 🎓 Ask opens the in-app tutor for that sentence, and ✓ Check (inside ✍️ Try) has the tutor judge your translation by meaning — each flagged error gets a why?. Set it up once via 🎓 Tutor in the top bar. Keyboard: ↑/↓ move · E English · L listen · S slow · N note · T try · A ask · Esc stop.
🎓 In-app tutor — answers questions about the focused sentence using Claude (Haiku). It runs on your own Anthropic API key, pay-per-use: a typical question costs a fraction of a cent.
Your key is stored only in this browser — it is never part of the website itself. Create one at console.anthropic.com (API keys), and set a monthly spend limit there for peace of mind.
